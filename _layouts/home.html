Practicals

The second half of this course will be a series of workshops covering applications in phylogenetic methods for macroevolution. Please come to class prepared to complete the tutorial with your own laptop. (If you do not have access to a laptop, please contact the instructors and one may be provided for you for use during class time.)

Grades
